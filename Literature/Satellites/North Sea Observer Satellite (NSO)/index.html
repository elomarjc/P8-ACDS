Content of the NSO CD-ROM

This CD-ROM is part of a project in Control Engineering concerning the development of a
robust disturbance rejecting attitude controller for a small satellite called The North Sea Observer (NSO). 
The project was developed during the proceedings of a master thesis at Aalborg University, called:

Robust Disturbance Rejecting Attitude Control - for the North Sea Observer Satellite.

10th semester students at the Department of Control Engineering at Aalborg University, 2006.

Martin Green, Martin Nygaard Kragelund and Mads Sletten Kristensen, 06gr1032

Software:
Simulator - The simulator for the NSO (Matlab/Simulink-files).
Calculations - Contains calculation/dimensioning files used in the modeling (Matlab-files).
Movies:
Movie - Is a simulation movie of the NSO in orbit tracking Aalborg (AVI-file, dir).
Literature:
References - The references and information sources used in the project (pdf-format).
Report - The report to which the CD-ROM belong (pdf-file, dir).
Programs:
Acrobat Reader - Acrobat reader.
